About Us

Empowering Communities Through Better Healthcare

Zoonie is a cloud-based healthcare platform that has been operating since 2024 under the supervision, guidance, and financial support of Om Shri Siddhi Vinayak Foundation, Prayagraj. The Foundation was registered in Prayagraj on 14 September 2010 and has been serving society since its establishment, with a special focus on supporting poor and middle-class communities. The primary objective behind the establishment of Zoonie is to make quality healthcare more affordable and accessible for everyone by helping reduce the cost of medical treatment. The platform also aims to simplify the healthcare process by minimizing the involvement of intermediaries and promoting direct access to healthcare services.

Bringing Healthcare Closer To Every Community

We believe that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right. Through innovative healthcare programs, awareness campaigns, technology-driven solutions, and community engagement, we strive to improve lives across urban and rural regions of Uttar Pradesh.

Front Side
Back Side

The S.F. Health Pragati Card is a unique women-focused healthcare and empowerment program designed to support both the health and economic well-being of families. This special combo card is available exclusively for women and is built around the vision of strengthening the social and financial position of women in society.

🏥 Healthcare Protection for the Entire Family

Through the Pragati Card, eligible families can receive healthcare support during medical emergencies. The program aims to provide financial assistance for healthcare needs, helping families access timely medical care when it matters most.

  • ✓ Coverage support for family healthcare needs
  • ✓ Medical emergency assistance
  • ✓ Improved healthcare accessibility
  • ✓ Family-focused healthcare benefits

👩 Women's Empowerment & Self-Employment Support

The Pragati Card goes beyond healthcare. It is designed to encourage women to become financially independent by supporting small business and self-employment opportunities. The initiative aims to help women develop sustainable income sources that can improve their family's economic stability.

  • ✓ Support for small business initiatives
  • ✓ Street food and snack stalls
  • ✓ Vegetable and fruit vending
  • ✓ Tea stalls and beverage businesses
  • ✓ Tailoring and stitching services
  • ✓ Mobile and wheel-based businesses
  • ✓ Other local self-employment opportunities

🌱 Creating Social Impact

When women become financially empowered, it strengthens families and communities. A woman who contributes to family income often gains greater confidence, decision-making ability, and social recognition. Economic independence can also help reduce social vulnerabilities and create a more secure future for the entire family.

  • ✓ Promotes financial independence
  • ✓ Improves family stability
  • ✓ Encourages entrepreneurship
  • ✓ Supports community development
  • ✓ Enhances social confidence and dignity

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to provide every woman with a platform for better healthcare access and economic growth. Through the S.F. Health Pragati Card, we strive to support women in becoming healthier, stronger, and more financially empowered while contributing to the overall development of their families.
